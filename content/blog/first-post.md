Welcome to my personal site. For years, my focus has been on building software, managing API integrations, and leading teams to solve complex technical challenges. But writing has always been the parallel thread running through my life.

Writing a memoir requires a structured approach not unlike designing a system architecture. You establish the core patterns, connect disparate layers of memory, and draft passages to express complex ideas. 

In this space, I will write about the intersections between SaaS, AdTech, scaling implementations, and the literary craft of storytelling. I hope you will join me on this journey.
